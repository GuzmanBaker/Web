Why we're Different


We're committed to providing accessible, high-quality mental health care for the whole family. Our therapists are specially trained to bring unconscious patterns into your conscious awareness. As you become aware of what was previously unconscious to you, we help you interrupt these patterns and relate to them with a sense of empowered agency. While active listening and empathy are essential, we recognize that meaningful and enduring change comes from challenging you to think and exist differently with feedback that is compassionate, honest, and actionable. One of the many things that sets us apart from most therapists is that we provide practical skills that allow you to take tangible action steps to overcome your mental health challenges and reach your goals.

All Ages Welcome

Flexible Scheduling

Therapy shouldn’t add to your stress. We offer evening and weekend appointments, plus secure telehealth options, so you can access care when and where it works for you.

Compassionate Candor

Empathy is our baseline, but growth requires honesty. We challenge you to interrupt old patterns and view your life through a new, empowered lens.

Tools, Not Just Talk

Insight is only half the battle. We equip you with tangible, practical skills you can use immediately to manage symptoms and navigate real-world challenges.

Evidence-Based Care

We rely on science, not guesswork. Our clinicians specialize in proven modalities—including CBT, DBT, and ACT—to effectively treat ADHD, Anxiety, Depression, and more.

Our Services

We are proud to offer a comprehensive suite of therapeutic services tailored to support you at every step of your mental health journey.

Individual Therapy

A safe, confidential space to work through life’s hurdles. We tailor our approach to your needs, helping you move from "surviving" to thriving. Evidence-based, one-on-one sessions are tailored to your unique history and goals, helping you process personal challenges and build lasting resilience.

Family Therapy

Shift from conflict to understanding. We help families break negative cycles and learn how to communicate effectively again. Interventions utilized strengthen bonds and improve communication. We help you navigate complex dynamics to resolve conflict and restore connection.

Child & Adolescent Therapy

Helping kids and teens navigate big emotions. We provide a supportive environment for young people to express themselves and learn coping skills. Specialized care for children (ages 6+) and teens. We use age-appropriate techniques to support emotional regulation and healthy development.

Psychological Assessments

Understanding is the first step toward healing. We provide professional testing for ADHD, Anxiety, and Depression to help you find the right path forward. Get clarity on the root cause of your symptoms. We offer comprehensive evaluations for ADHD, Anxiety, and Depression to guide effective treatment.

Safety Planning & Risk Assessment

You are not alone in the dark. We offer professional risk assessments and collaborative safety planning to keep you or your loved one safe. Compassionate, clinical evaluation of risk. We provide immediate safety planning, intervention strategies, and support for at-risk individuals.

Caregiver & Sibling Support

Caregivers need care, too. We offer validation and strategies for those supporting a family member with medical or behavioral complexities. We provide a dedicated space to navigate the unique emotional weight of caregiving. We support those managing the medical, behavioral, or developmental complexities of a loved one.

What to Expect

Getting started with therapy is simple. Here's how the process works.

Submit Your Request

Our Therapy Team

Guzman & Baker Behavioral Health Services is a collective group of diverse mental health practitioners who share a common goal of expanding access and education for mental health care. Our collaborative model ensures that families receive evidence-based, culturally sensitive treatment tailored to each individuals unique needs. We bridge the gap between primary care and specialized mental health services, creating seamless pathways to healing for patients and their families. Our therapists are highly vetted, not only for their clinical acumen, but for who they are, and their unrelenting dedication to their own personal and professional growth.
